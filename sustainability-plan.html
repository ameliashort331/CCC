Sustainability Plan Follow-Up

The City of College Park voted to adopt and implement a 2015 plan that maps out a strategy for pursuing the goals of sustainability. Embedded in the plan are a number of measures that city operations could implement that would reduce the jurisdiction’s carbon footprint. Most if not all cost money, and might benefit from a financing strategy and/or a business process for implementation.
